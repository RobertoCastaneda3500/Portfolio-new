About Me

I was born and raised in the city of Chicago, Illinois. Attended a public elementary school and a charter high school in the city. Some of my favorite things to do in the city are visiting sports stadiums, hangout in downtown, and enjoy the lake at Lake Michigan.

After years in the city, I went off to college at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. After 4 years, I graduated with a bachelor of science in Computer Systems Technology. GO REDBIRDS!

After graduating college, I was interested in web development. One of my professors has inspired me to become a front-end developer after taking her course. One of my goals is to become a senior full stack developer in the next 5 to 7 years.
