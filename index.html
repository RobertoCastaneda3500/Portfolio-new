About Me

I was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. Attended a public elementary school and a charter high school in the city. One of my favorite things to do in the city is visit sports stadiums, hangout in downtown, and visit Lake Michigan.

After years in the city, I went off to college to Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. There I did four years of computers systems technology and graduated with a bachelors of science. GO REDBIRDS!

After graduating college, I was interested in web development. One of my professors had inspired me to become a front-end developer after taking her required class. One of my goals is to become a senior full stack developer in the next 5-7 years or sooner.
