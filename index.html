About Me

I was born and raised in the city of Chicago, Illinois. Attended a public elementary school and a charter high school in the city. Some of my favorite things to do in the city is visit sports stadiums, hangout in downtown, and visit Lake Michigan.

After years in the city, I went off to college to Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. After 4 years, I graduated with a bachelors of science in Computer Systems Technology. GO REDBIRDS!

After graduating college, I was interested in web development. One of my professors had inspired me to become a front-end developer after taking her course. One of my goals is to become a senior full stack developer in the next 5 to 7 years.
